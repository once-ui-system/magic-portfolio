## Overview

Red Casket Club is a music and brand persona project that creates a complete audiovisual world. The project combines original music production with visual identity development, demonstrating the power of cohesive creative direction across multiple media.

## Key Features

- **Original Music Production**: Composed and produced original tracks that define the sonic signature of the Red Casket Club persona.
- **Visual Identity System**: Developed logos, color palettes, typography, and mood boards that create a cohesive visual world.
- **Brand Narrative**: Crafted the backstory and conceptual framework that gives depth to the persona.
- **DAW Workflow**: Established efficient production workflows for consistent sound design and music creation.

## Technologies Used

- **Digital Audio Workstation**: For music production, mixing, and mastering.
- **Sound Design Tools**: For creating unique sonic textures and atmospheres.
- **Visual Design**: Adobe Creative Suite for brand identity development.
- **AI Integration**: Suno and other generative tools for exploring creative possibilities.

## Outcome

Red Casket Club demonstrates the potential for creating immersive brand experiences through the careful integration of sound and visual design, serving as a template for future persona and brand development projects.
