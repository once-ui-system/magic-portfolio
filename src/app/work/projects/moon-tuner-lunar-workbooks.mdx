## Overview

Moon Tuner is a platform for personalized lunar workbooks that integrate journaling, ritual frameworks, and celestial tracking. The project bridges ancient lunar wisdom with modern digital tools, creating accessible guides for intentional living aligned with natural cycles.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Lunar Guides**: Developed customizable workbook templates that adapt to individual birth charts and current lunar phases.
- **Ritual Framework Integration**: Created structured ritual frameworks for each lunar phase, integrating journaling prompts, reflection exercises, and actionable practices.
- **Calendar & Tracking System**: Built intuitive tracking systems that help users follow lunar cycles and document their personal experiences over time.
- **Visual Design System**: Established a cohesive visual language using celestial imagery and minimalist design principles for clear, inspiring content.

## Technologies Used

- **Google Docs & Notion**: For collaborative content development and workbook structuring.
- **InDesign & Canva**: For professional layout and visual design of physical and digital workbooks.
- **Web Platform (Next.js)**: For the moontuner.xyz digital experience and workbook delivery.

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was creating content that felt both spiritually meaningful and practically actionable. This required extensive research into lunar traditions across cultures while maintaining accessibility for modern audiences. User feedback was essential in refining the balance between depth and usability.

## Outcome

Moon Tuner has grown into a recognized platform in the intentional living space, serving users who want to align their daily practices with natural cycles. The workbooks have helped establish a community of practitioners who find value in lunar-aligned reflection and ritual.
