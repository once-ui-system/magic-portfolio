## Overview

The Cultivárot Tarot is a unique deck that reimagines traditional tarot archetypes through a contemporary lens. This project explores symbolic density, visual storytelling, and the intersection of ancient divination systems with modern creative expression.

## Key Features

- **Rewritten Archetypes**: Each card in the Major Arcana has been thoughtfully reinterpreted to resonate with contemporary experiences while honoring traditional meanings.
- **Symbolic Density**: The visual design incorporates multiple layers of symbolism, inviting deeper exploration with each viewing.
- **Guidebook Development**: Created comprehensive companion materials explaining the unique interpretations and suggested spreads for the deck.
- **Visual Language System**: Developed a cohesive aesthetic that bridges classical tarot imagery with modern design sensibilities.

## Technologies Used

- **Visual Design Tools**: Adobe Creative Suite for illustration and layout.
- **Research & Documentation**: Notion for archetype development and symbolic research.
- **Print Production**: Professional print preparation and quality control processes.

## Outcome

The Cultivárot Tarot represents a bridge between traditional esoteric systems and contemporary creative practice, demonstrating the potential for reinterpreting ancient symbolic systems for modern audiences.
