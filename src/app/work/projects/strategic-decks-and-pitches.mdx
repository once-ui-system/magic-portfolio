## Overview

A collection of strategic deliverables demonstrating expertise in brand strategy, pitch development, and systems thinking. These projects showcase the ability to translate complex ideas into clear, compelling narratives for diverse audiences.

## Key Features

- **Pitch Deck Development**: Created comprehensive pitch decks for startups, creative projects, and strategic initiatives.
- **Problem/Solution Frameworks**: Developed clear articulations of challenges and proposed solutions using visual storytelling.
- **Process Diagrams**: Designed visual representations of workflows, systems, and methodologies.
- **One-Pagers & Executive Summaries**: Crafted concise, high-impact documents for quick stakeholder comprehension.

## Approach

Each strategic document follows a methodology that combines:

- **Systems Thinking**: Understanding how different elements interconnect within the larger context.
- **Narrative Architecture**: Building compelling stories that guide readers through complex information.
- **Visual Communication**: Using design principles to enhance comprehension and engagement.
- **AI-Assisted Workflows**: Leveraging generative AI tools to accelerate research and content development.

## Technologies Used

- **Presentation Tools**: Keynote, Google Slides, Figma for deck creation.
- **AI Assistants**: OpenAI and Claude for research synthesis and content refinement.
- **Visual Design**: Canva and Adobe Creative Suite for graphics and layouts.
- **Documentation**: Notion for project management and content organization.

## Outcome

These strategic deliverables have helped clients secure funding, clarify their positioning, and communicate complex ideas effectively. The work demonstrates the value of combining creative direction with strategic thinking.
